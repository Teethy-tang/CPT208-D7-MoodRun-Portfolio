MOODRUN

Emotion-Aware Running Companion

Start Portfolio Quest Jump to Prototype Logic

Project Direction

Retro-game visual tone, clear reading flow, and a strict five-part structure.

Concept Core

MoodRun focuses on emotional wellbeing during running. Instead of only tracking speed and distance, the system responds to feelings like stress, low motivation, or high energy with adaptive guidance.

Design Principle

Keep the style playful and nostalgic (pixel-like accents and arcade-like cards), while ensuring content hierarchy remains readable for tutors and teammates.
